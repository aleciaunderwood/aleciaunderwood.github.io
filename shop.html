About This Book

Unbroken Promises: The Promise Behind Alecia’s Kitchen is not just a diner story. It is a story about the pieces of life that stay with us — the people we lose, the memories we carry, and the promises that still matter years later.

Before Alecia’s Kitchen existed, Michael’s first version of the story centered on Jessica, their breakup, and the pain that eventually shaped the idea of Jessica’s Kitchen. Later, another memory came forward: an old childhood friend named Alecia, someone Michael lost touch with after his parents divorced.

Alecia Underwood became a fictional, AI-created character inspired by that memory — a voice full of heart, truth, humor, and enough sarcasm to keep the coffee nervous.
