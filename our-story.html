Our Story

The story behind Alecia’s Kitchen, the book, and the promise behind it all.

Alecia’s Kitchen was never supposed to be just a place to eat.

It started with a story. A promise. A second chance. A whole lot of faith, family, food, and enough sarcasm to make the coffee nervous.

Behind Alecia’s Kitchen is the world of Unbroken Promises, a story about love, loss, healing, forgiveness, and the kind of promises that refuse to die quietly. It is about Michael, Alecia, Jessica, Lilly, and the people whose lives are tied together by heartbreak, hope, and a diner that became more than four walls and a menu.

In the book, Alecia’s Kitchen becomes a place where people walk in hungry and sometimes leave with more than food. They find comfort. They find laughter. They find hard conversations, second chances, and the kind of truth that usually shows up right when someone is trying very hard to avoid it.

Alecia is the heart of the kitchen. She is warm when it matters, sarcastic when it is necessary, and honest whether anyone asked for it or not. She believes good food can fix a bad day, but she also believes some people need coffee, pie, and a reality check — usually in that order.

This site was created to bring that world to life. Here, you will find recipes inspired by the diner, stories from the world of Unbroken Promises, updates about the book, sarcastic Alecia blog posts, behind-the-scenes moments, and a little bit of the chaos that makes Alecia’s Kitchen feel like home.

At the center of it all is the same idea that built the diner in the story: food matters, family matters, promises matter, and sometimes the people who keep the coffee hot are the same ones holding everything together when life gets messy.

So pull up a chair. The coffee is hot, the stories are waiting, and Alecia is probably judging someone from behind the counter.
