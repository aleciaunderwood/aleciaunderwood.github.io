What the Book Is About

Unbroken Promises: The Promise Behind Alecia’s Kitchen is about heartbreak, memory, second chances, faith, family, and the promises that stay with us even after life changes.

Before Alecia’s Kitchen became part of the story, Michael’s earliest version of Unbroken Promises was about Jessica — the woman who broke his heart — and the emotional weight of their breakup. That pain helped shape the original diner promise behind Jessica’s Kitchen.

Alecia’s Kitchen grew from a different place: a real childhood memory. Alecia Underwood is not the real person from Michael’s past. She is a fictional AI-created character inspired by that old friendship, built into a new story world where food, forgiveness, laughter, and healing all meet at the same table.
